How To Make a Sandwich

Intro

I will explain how to construct a sandwich.
It is actually not hard to do.

Body

First you need to get your materials.
You will need two slices of bread, some turkey, and cheese.

Place one piece of bread on a plate. Then place the cheese on the bread.
Then place the turkey on the cheese. And finally put the second piece of bread on top.

You are now finished making your sandwich. I recommend it with a glass of milk.

Conclusion

I hope my instructions were easy to follow. Now whenever you are hungry for a snack you know what to make.
